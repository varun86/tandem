# Prompts

Copy and paste these prompts into Tandem in order.

## Prompt 1: Initial Risk Scan
> "I have a draft employment agreement in reasonable markdown format. Please scan `EMPLOYMENT_AGREEMENT_DRAFT.md` and identify any clauses that might be legally unenforceable or pose a high risk to the employee. Specifically, look at the non-compete and IP assignment clauses."

## Prompt 2: Case Summarization
> "Read `CASE_NOTES_SMITH_V_JONES.md`. Summarize the key facts of the case and the arguments for both sides. What is the central legal issue effectively deciding this case?"

## Prompt 3: Contract Comparison
> "Compare the IP assignment clause in the `EMPLOYMENT_AGREEMENT_DRAFT.md` with the arguments presented by the defendant in `CASE_NOTES_SMITH_V_JONES.md`. Does the contract language support the employer's case?"

## Prompt 4: Drafting a Memo
> "Using the `LEGAL_RESEARCH_MEMO_TEMPLATE.md`, draft a formal legal memorandum to the Senior Partner analyzing the 'Smith v. Jones' case. Use the facts from the case notes and your analysis of the employment agreement to recommend a strategy for the client."

## Prompt 5: Client Dashboard (HTML)
> "Create a 'Litigation Dashboard' HTML file that visualizes the status of the 'Smith v. Jones' case. It should include:
> 1. A timeline of events.
> 2. A probability of success meter (estimate based on facts).
> 3. Key upcoming dates.
> 4. A summary of the risk factors.
> Save this as `outputs/litigation_dashboard.html`."
